What helped me learn as a newbie to coding?

When it comes to coding, it can be hard to jump into it and begin learning because new things develop everyday. As, computer scientists, we must be ready to learn and adapt to the dynamic world around us. I know I am not alone when it comes to wondering on the best resources to stay on top of my field. I have provided different youtube playlists, udemy courses, and websites that have helped push me to further learn about coding. Here are some of my most helpful ones!

Udemy Courses

As a student at Oakland University, we emphsized a lot on Java learning. When the Covid-19 pandemic hit, I found it more difficult to interact with my Professors and to learn everything to its full potential. I decided to take initiative and discover a new way to learn code without school. Udemy courses allowed to get the basic foundations for multiple languages and I still continue to use use each course when wanting to learn new things.
